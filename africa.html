About Africa

Africa, the world's second-largest and second-most populous continent, is a land of incredible diversity and captivating beauty. With its vast landscapes, rich history, and diverse cultures, Africa has always been a source of fascination and wonder. From the mighty Sahara Desert to the lush rainforests of the Congo Basin, Africa offers a breathtaking array of natural wonders. Its wildlife is unparalleled, with majestic creatures like elephants, lions, and giraffes roaming across its plains and savannahs. Africa's vibrant cultures and traditions are as diverse as its geography, with over 2,000 distinct languages spoken and a myriad of ethnic groups and tribes. From the ancient pyramids of Egypt to the bustling markets of Marrakech and the vibrant rhythms of West African music, Africa's heritage is steeped in ancient traditions and artistic expressions. Exploring Africa is an invitation to embark on a journey of discovery, immersing oneself in its breathtaking landscapes, encountering its incredible wildlife, and experiencing the warmth and hospitality of its people.

Attractions in Africa

Pyramids of Egypt

The Pyramids of Egypt are ancient monumental structures built as tombs for the pharaohs and their queens. They are located on the western bank of the Nile River near Cairo. The most famous pyramid is the Great Pyramid of Giza, constructed for Pharaoh Khufu. These pyramids were built during the Old Kingdom period around 2600 BC and stand as a testament to the advanced engineering and architectural prowess of the ancient Egyptians.

Serengeti National Park

Serengeti National Park is a world-renowned wildlife sanctuary located in Tanzania, East Africa. Spanning an area of approximately 14,750 square kilometers (5,700 square miles), it is one of the most iconic and diverse ecosystems on the planet.
